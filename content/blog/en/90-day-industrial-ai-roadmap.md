# A 90-day industrial AI roadmap

An industrial AI project does not need to begin with a massive transformation. It can start with 90 focused days to prove value, learn, and decide whether to scale.

## Days 1 to 15: use case selection

The first step is choosing a problem with measurable impact.

A good candidate:

- Has clear operational pain.
- Has data or images available.
- Has an internal owner.
- Can be measured before and after.
- Does not require changing the whole plant.

## Days 16 to 35: data and infrastructure

This phase reviews what exists and what is missing.

Activities:

- Sample collection.
- Data quality validation.
- Baseline metric definition.
- Integration review.
- Pilot design.

## Days 36 to 70: functional prototype

The goal is to build something that works with real data, even if it is not yet ready for full production.

Validate:

- Sufficient precision.
- Operational workflow.
- Latency.
- User experience.
- Risks and exceptions.

## Days 71 to 90: operational pilot

The pilot should prove value under controlled real conditions.

Expected outputs:

- Before/after metric.
- Improvement list.
- Scaling requirements.
- ROI estimate.
- Next-phase decision.

## Conclusion

Ninety days are enough to know whether an industrial AI use case has a future. The key is avoiding generic pilots and focusing on a metric the operation recognizes as valuable.
